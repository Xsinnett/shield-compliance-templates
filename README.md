# Shield Compliance — Privacy Policy Templates

A collection of privacy policy templates and compliance checklists 
for small businesses, built as part of Shield Compliance 
(https://shieldcompliance.carrd.co).

## What's Included

| Regulation | Who It Applies To |
|------------|------------------|
| GDPR | Businesses handling EU resident data |
| CCPA | Businesses handling California resident data |
| HIPAA | Healthcare-related businesses |
| PIPEDA | Businesses handling Canadian resident data |

## How to Use
Each folder contains a ready-to-customize privacy policy template 
and a compliance checklist. Download the relevant files for your 
jurisdiction, fill in your business details, and review with a 
qualified attorney before publishing.
