## 1. Does the CCPA Apply to You?
- [ ] Your business operates in California or serves California residents
- [ ] You meet at least ONE of the following thresholds:
  - [ ] Annual gross revenue exceeds $25 million
  - [ ] Buy, sell, or share personal info of 100,000+ consumers or households
  - [ ] Derive 50% or more of annual revenue from selling personal information

*Note: Even if you don't meet these thresholds, following CCPA 
best practices builds trust with customers.*

---

## 2. Data Inventory
- [ ] Identified all categories of personal information you collect
- [ ] Documented where personal information comes from
- [ ] Documented why you collect each category of information
- [ ] Documented who you share personal information with
- [ ] Documented how long you retain each category of information
- [ ] Updated your data inventory in the last 12 months

## 3. Privacy Policy
- [ ] Privacy policy is publicly available on your website
- [ ] Privacy policy is accessible from your homepage
- [ ] Policy lists all categories of personal information collected
- [ ] Policy explains the purpose for collecting each category
- [ ] Policy lists all third parties you share information with
- [ ] Policy explains consumer rights under the CCPA
- [ ] Policy includes instructions for submitting rights requests
- [ ] Policy includes a "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" 
      section if applicable
- [ ] Policy has been updated within the last 12 months

## 4. Consumer Rights Requests
- [ ] Process in place to receive and verify rights requests
- [ ] At least two methods available to submit requests 
      (email, phone, web form)
- [ ] Able to respond to requests within 45 days
- [ ] Process in place to extend response time by additional 45 days 
      if needed with notice to consumer
- [ ] Process in place to provide information covering the past 
      12 months
- [ ] Process in place to delete personal information upon request
- [ ] Process in place to correct inaccurate information upon request
- [ ] Not charging fees for processing rights requests
- [ ] Not discriminating against consumers who exercise their rights

## 5. Do Not Sell or Share
- [ ] Determined whether you sell or share personal information
- [ ] "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link on homepage 
      if applicable
- [ ] Process in place to honor opt-out requests within 15 days
- [ ] Not re-selling information from consumers who have opted out
- [ ] Contracts with third parties prohibit them from selling 
      information you share with them

## 6. Sensitive Personal Information
- [ ] Identified whether you collect sensitive personal information
- [ ] "Limit the Use of My Sensitive Personal Information" link on 
      homepage if applicable
- [ ] Process in place to limit use of sensitive information upon request
- [ ] Sensitive information only used for permitted purposes

## 7. Data Security
- [ ] Reasonable security measures in place to protect personal information
- [ ] Access to personal information restricted to those who need it
- [ ] Security measures reviewed and updated regularly
- [ ] Process in place to detect and respond to data breaches
- [ ] Process in place to notify affected consumers of breaches

## 8. Service Providers & Third Parties
- [ ] Identified all service providers who handle personal information
- [ ] Written contracts in place with all service providers
- [ ] Contracts prohibit service providers from selling personal 
      information
- [ ] Contracts prohibit service providers from retaining or using 
      information beyond the service provided
- [ ] Verified service providers are CCPA compliant

## 9. Children's Privacy
- [ ] Not collecting personal information from children under 13 
      without parental consent
- [ ] Not selling personal information of consumers under 16 without 
      opt-in consent
- [ ] Process in place to obtain opt-in consent from consumers 
      aged 13-15

## 10. Employee & HR Data
- [ ] Employees informed about what personal information is collected 
      and why
- [ ] Employee privacy notice provided at or before point of collection
- [ ] Process in place to handle employee rights requests
