## 7. Your Rights Under the CCPA

As a California resident you have the following rights:

- **Right to Know** — request disclosure of the personal information 
  we have collected about you over the past 12 months
- **Right to Delete** — request deletion of personal information we 
  have collected, subject to certain exceptions
- **Right to Correct** — request correction of inaccurate personal 
  information we hold about you
- **Right to Opt-Out** — opt out of the sale or sharing of your 
  personal information
- **Right to Limit Use** — limit our use of your sensitive personal 
  information
- **Right to Non-Discrimination** — we will not discriminate against 
  you for exercising any of your CCPA rights
