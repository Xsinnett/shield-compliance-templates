# GDPR Compliance Checklist

*Prepared by Shield Compliance | shieldcompliance.carrd.co*  
*This checklist is for informational purposes only and does not 
constitute legal advice.*

---

## 1. Lawful Basis for Processing
- [ ] Identified a lawful basis for every type of data you process
- [ ] Documented your lawful basis in your privacy policy
- [ ] Obtained clear consent where consent is your lawful basis
- [ ] Made it as easy to withdraw consent as it is to give it

## 2. Privacy Policy
- [ ] Privacy policy is publicly available on your website
- [ ] Policy explains what data you collect and why
- [ ] Policy explains how long you keep data
- [ ] Policy explains who you share data with
- [ ] Policy lists all user rights under GDPR
- [ ] Policy includes your contact information
- [ ] Policy is written in clear, plain language

## 3. Data Collection
- [ ] Only collecting data that is necessary for your purpose
- [ ] Informing users what data is being collected at point of collection
- [ ] Not collecting special category data without explicit consent
- [ ] Keeping records of what data you collect and why

## 4. User Rights
- [ ] Process in place to handle Subject Access Requests (SARs)
- [ ] Able to respond to SARs within 30 days
- [ ] Process in place to delete user data upon request
- [ ] Process in place to correct inaccurate data upon request
- [ ] Process in place to export user data in portable format
- [ ] Process in place to restrict processing upon request

## 5. Data Security
- [ ] Personal data is stored securely
- [ ] Access to personal data is restricted to those who need it
- [ ] Data is encrypted where appropriate
- [ ] Secure passwords and two-factor authentication in place
- [ ] Process in place to detect and respond to data breaches
- [ ] Able to notify authorities of a breach within 72 hours
- [ ] Able to notify affected individuals of a breach without undue delay

## 6. Third Parties & Data Sharing
- [ ] Identified all third parties you share data with
- [ ] Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) in place with all third parties
- [ ] Third parties are GDPR compliant
- [ ] Not selling personal data to third parties

## 7. Cookies
- [ ] Cookie banner in place on website
- [ ] Users can accept or decline non-essential cookies
- [ ] Cookie policy is accessible and up to date
- [ ] Only setting essential cookies before consent is given

## 8. Data Retention
- [ ] Defined retention periods for each type of data you hold
- [ ] Retention periods documented in your privacy policy
- [ ] Process in place to delete data when retention period expires
- [ ] Not keeping data longer than necessary

## 9. International Transfers
- [ ] Identified whether you transfer data outside the EEA
- [ ] Appropriate safeguards in place for any international transfers
- [ ] Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) in place where required

## 10. Record Keeping
- [ ] Maintaining records of all processing activities
- [ ] Records include what data is processed, why, and by whom
- [ ] Records are kept up to date
